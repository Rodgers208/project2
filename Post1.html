Why Your Volvo Won’t Start in the Cold

Published on Aug 5, 2025

← Back to Blog

Cold weather can seriously affect your vehicle. Common reasons include:

  • Dead battery – cold drains battery charge
  • Thickened engine oil
  • Fuel line freeze
  • Old spark plugs or weak starter

To prevent this, keep your battery charged, use winter-grade oil, and park in a warm space if possible.
