5 Tools Every DIY Mechanic Needs

Published on Aug 3, 2025

If you're serious about working on your own car, you’ll need these essential tools:

  1. Socket Set – For loosening and tightening bolts with ease.
  2. Torque Wrench – Ensures bolts are tightened to the right specs.
  3. OBD2 Scanner – Diagnoses engine and sensor issues.
  4. Jack and Jack Stands – Safely lift your car off the ground.
  5. Multimeter – Troubleshoot electrical problems fast.

With these tools, you'll be better equipped to tackle most repairs and maintenance jobs confidently.
